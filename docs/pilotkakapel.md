# CHAT KAKAPEL PILOT PROGRAM
## Cultural Heritage Asset Token - Kakapel National Monument Initiative

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The CHAT Kakapel Pilot Program is a 6-month proof-of-concept initiative designed to preserve and tokenize the endangered cultural heritage of the Kakapel National Monument region in Western Kenya. This pilot will validate CHAT's technical infrastructure, economic model, and community engagement framework before broader African expansion.

### Pilot Objectives

1. **Preserve 100+ Cultural Assets** from Kakapel region (rock art, oral histories, traditional practices)
2. **Establish Local Creator Economy** with 50 community members earning $100-500 in Year 1
3. **Validate Technical Stack** (Hedera timestamping, Ethereum NFT minting, AI verification)
4. **Build Community Governance** with local validator council and DOVU partnership
5. **Generate $10,000+ Conservation Fund** for Kakapel Monument preservation
6. **Demonstrate Sustainable Model** replicable across 50+ African heritage sites

### Why Kakapel?

**Critical Heritage Site:**
- Ancient rock art dating back 2,000+ years facing environmental degradation
- Rich oral traditions from Teso, Luo, and Kalenjin communities at risk of extinction
- Limited documentation and preservation infrastructure
- Active community interest in cultural preservation

**Strategic Advantages:**
- Proximity to Busia/Kisumu urban centers (connectivity)
- Partnership with DOVU (established cultural preservation organization)
- Government recognition as National Monument (regulatory support)
- Diverse cultural assets (visual, oral, ceremonial)
- Strong community leadership structure

**Measurable Impact:**
- Immediate: Digital preservation of at-risk cultural knowledge
- Economic: $5,000-10,000 direct earnings for local creators
- Environmental: Reduced physical tourism pressure on fragile rock art
- Social: Youth engagement with traditional knowledge
- Scalable: Validated model for 50+ African sites

#### Submission Workflow (Detailed)

**Step 1: Creator Preparation**
- Attend training workshop
- Download CHAT mobile app
- Create wallet (or use custodial option)
- Stake 100 CHAT (refunded if approved)

**Step 2: Content Capture**
- Follow documentation standards
- Record metadata (title, description, context)
- Set permissions (public, restricted, sacred)
- Preview before submission

**Step 3: AI Screening (<2 seconds)**
- Whisper: Language detection + transcription
- Vision Transformer: Image authenticity
- CLIP: Content classification
- Result: Auto-approve (>85%) or flag for review

**Step 4: Validator Review (If Flagged)**
- DOVU guardian reviews (24-48 hours)
- 2 local elders validate (cultural accuracy)
- 3-of-5 multisig approval required
- Feedback provided if rejected

**Step 5: Hedera Timestamping**
- Asset hash recorded on Hedera
- Immutable proof-of-creation
- Conservation fund reserved (20%)
- Submission stake refunded

**Step 6: Ethereum Minting**
- ERC-1155 NFT minted
- Metadata URI (IPFS hash)
- Royalty configuration embedded
- Marketplace listing activated

**Step 7: Creator Notification**
- SMS/email confirmation
- NFT ID and marketplace link
- Earnings dashboard updated
- Social share tools provided
